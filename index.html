COD Warzone

A 150-player battle royale mode that leverages 17 years of Call of Duty gun and map design experience, Warzone is an addictive last-man-standing survival shooter. Three players team up to take down everyone they encounter, in the meantime picking up weapons, ammo, grenades, and armor strewn about the apocalyptic map.
